About Me

Hello! I'm Niraj Kumar, a second-year B.Tech Computer Science student at Lovely Professional University. My passion for technology and innovation has led me to delve deeply into the world of web development, where I'm constantly exploring new ways to create engaging and efficient web experiences.

Since starting my journey in computer science, I've developed a strong foundation in programming and problem-solving. My coursework has provided me with a solid understanding of algorithms, data structures, and software engineering principles. However, it was my curiosity and enthusiasm for web technologies that truly sparked my interest and drove me to specialize in web development.

Over the past year, I've been honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with frameworks like React and Node.js. I love bringing ideas to life through code, and I'm always eager to learn new techniques and tools to improve my craft.
